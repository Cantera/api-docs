Detailed Description

Compound functors implement expressions that are composed of other functors.

The following compound functor types are implemented:

  • "sum" (class Sum1),
  • "diff" (class Diff1),
  • "product" (class Product1),
  • "ratio" (class Ratio1),
  • "composite" (class Composite1),
